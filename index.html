The Inverted Pendulum Lesson

1. Inertia (Lagging): When the cart accelerates right, the bob lags behind, appearing to tilt left from the cart's frame.

2. Correction: To balance, the cart must move in the same direction the ball is falling to "catch" it.

3. PID Control: Proportional reacts to tilt; Derivative acts as a brake to prevent high-speed overshoot.
